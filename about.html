How Franciele leads

A professional presence, not a rushed decision.

Franciele uses the consultation to understand what you want, what your skin is showing, and whether your timing and expectations support treatment.

Listening has a purpose

The conversation gathers goals, history, routine, sensitivity, prior procedures, and timing.

Explanation lowers pressure

You should understand why an option is being discussed before you choose anything.

Restraint builds trust

Not every treatment is right for every person, and not every concern needs action now.

Biomedicine-led care

Clinical responsibility can still feel warm.

The Sofiati atmosphere is botanical and feminine, but the care logic is practical: assessment before recommendation, preparation before procedure, and honest expectation-setting before commitment.
