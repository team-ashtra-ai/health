07

REVIEW TRANSPARENCY

Why Accuracy Matters

  • Only genuine reviews or permission-approved patient feedback should be published;
  • Spelling may be lightly corrected only when meaning remains unchanged;
  • A full name or photograph should appear only with clear permission;
  • Positive experiences do not guarantee another person’s clinical result;
  • Google rating and review totals should be read directly from the current public profile.
08

SHARE YOUR EXPERIENCE

Share Your Experience

A useful review does not need to be long or exaggerated. Specific, truthful feedback is often the most helpful.

  • How you felt during the consultation;
  • Whether the treatment was explained clearly;
  • What helped you feel comfortable;
  • How preparation or aftercare was communicated;
  • What you valued about the environment;
  • Whether the experience matched what was explained.
