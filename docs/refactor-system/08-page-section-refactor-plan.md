# Page and Section Refactor Plan

Date: 2026-06-26

## Global Page Rules

- Every page must keep a clear H1, descriptive metadata, canonical URL, internal links, ethical disclaimers where relevant and a consultation/contact path.
- Required content may repeat in meaning, but section order, visual rhythm, imagery, CTA placement and card treatment must vary by concept.
- Legal/medical/beauty copy changes require human review unless they are purely formatting or approved label fixes.
- Each page should have at least one natural internal link cluster that supports the visitor journey without dumping links at the bottom.
- Mobile layouts must be designed for 360 and 390px, not just stacked desktop.

## CTA Placement Rules

- First CTA can appear in hero only if paired with educational alternate route.
- Service pages should explain evaluation/suitability before pushing consultation.
- Legal/privacy pages should not sell aggressively.
- Contact and consultation pages should keep route choice clear and low friction.
- Never imply treatment is suitable without evaluation.

## Responsive Rules

- At 360/390px: no text overlap, no nav/control overflow, no floating widget covering primary CTA/form controls.
- At 768px: tablet menu/header must be intentionally chosen.
- At 1024px: desktop nav must not wrap unless the concept deliberately switches to compact/menu mode and still exposes essential routes.
- At 1366/1440px: header and footer alignment must feel premium and stable.
