## 3. Design DNA
Signature uses Signature Personal Mark: split balance grid with rounded image bracket. The site rhythm is balanced two-column sections with offset media. It must feel premium, botanical, calm, personal, and distinctly different from the other 49 concepts.

## 4. Design notes
- Hero strategy: split balance grid with rounded image bracket.
- Section silhouettes: balanced two-column sections with offset media.
- Image rhythm: cutout, service visual, texture, form frame.
- Motion language: split panels settle without layout shift.
- Must not look like: symmetry that makes every page feel identical.

## 5. Brand foundation
Sofiati shares one ethical brand foundation: cuidado, confianca, seguranca, naturalidade, premium calm, real-photo-led storytelling, and professional guidance without pressure.

## 13. Asset notes
All production media for Signature is local to assets/generated/concept-48 and is derived from assets/photos and assets/brand. assets/brand-photos is not present in this branch, so transparent portrait/brand sources in assets/brand are used for portrait treatments. Meaningful photos need descriptive alt text. Decorative icons may use empty alt text. Keep images light, crop faces safely on mobile, and replace assets only with equal-quality Sofiati brand material.

## 14. Asset plan
Hero, human-trust, service, education, support, navigation, footer, form, responsive, and social preview moments use the generated concept-48 asset set. The same Sofiati source material may repeat, but crop, frame, colour treatment, section role, and mobile presentation must change for Signature.

## 15. Portrait/photo usage plan
Use generated portrait crops from assets/generated/concept-48/portraits and generated hero crops from assets/generated/concept-48/heroes. Keep Franciele visible on mobile and never darken or blur the portrait beyond recognition.

## 16. Botanical/decorative asset usage plan
Use concept-48 botanical SVGs as dividers, stamps, corner details, and section accents. They support rhythm only; they must not cover text, buttons, or faces.

## 17. Background/texture usage plan
Use concept-48 generated backgrounds behind statement and support sections. Texture opacity must stay low enough for WCAG-friendly contrast.

## 18. Form asset usage plan
Use assets/generated/concept-48/forms assets in consultation and contact CTA sections to frame the action without creating pressure.

## 19. Animation/motion plan
split panels settle without layout shift. All motion must respect reduced-motion preferences.

## 20. Journal asset plan
Journal and blog sections use three thumbnail assets and present education as a premium magazine preview, not a link dump.

## 21. Service asset plan
Care, Skin, Laser, and Results use distinct service SVGs with short explanatory copy. No equipment claims are invented.

## 22. Content direction
Copy stays short, ethical, image-led, premium, feminine, calm, and conversion-focused without pressure.

## 23. Same-content rules
The meaning may match the Sofiati foundation, but Signature must vary layout signatures, section order, asset rhythm, CTA rhythm, partial strategy, and mobile presentation.
