# 40-noble — Mature Luxury

## Visual Thesis

A mature dignified luxury site with serious premium presence.

## Colour Thesis

Deep sage, ink, ivory, bronze. Minimal blush. Strong mature contrast.

## Systems

- Header: `quiet-portrait-header`
- Mobile: `magazine-drawer`
- Footer: `dark-flagship`
- Homepage anatomy: `conversation-field`
- Internal page anatomy: `technical-table`
- Component strategy: technical-table with minimal-arrow calls to action
- CTA strategy: dark-anchor
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 5, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Deep noble hero
2. Ivory dignified trust
3. Sage service structure
4. Ink consultation band
5. Bronze reassurance statement
6. Ivory education section
7. Sage results ledger
8. Cream journal row
9. Deep CTA band
10. Noble dark footer

## All Other Pages

Use stately spacing, restrained colour, and strong hierarchy.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No playful blush
- No spa softness
- No overly decorative botanical motifs.
