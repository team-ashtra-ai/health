# 49-wisdom — Education and Trust

## Visual Thesis

A deeper education/trust site with long-form reading structure and responsible decision-making.

## Colour Thesis

Paper ivory, sage reading sections, bronze note markers, blush learning callouts, dark footer.

## Systems

- Header: `route-map-header`
- Mobile: `studio-card`
- Footer: `warm-reassurance`
- Homepage anatomy: `contact-bridge`
- Internal page anatomy: `process-rail`
- Component strategy: atelier-frame with dark-anchor calls to action
- CTA strategy: whatsapp-strip
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 5, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Paper wisdom hero
2. Sage trust reading
3. Cream care route index
4. Ivory consultation education
5. Blush learning callout
6. Sage topic cluster
7. Cream results explanation
8. Ivory journal library
9. Bronze learning CTA
10. Deep sage wisdom footer

## All Other Pages

Use reading sequences, FAQ clusters, journal bridges, and educational rhythm.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No aggressive conversion
- No superficial cards
- No dark luxury dominance.
