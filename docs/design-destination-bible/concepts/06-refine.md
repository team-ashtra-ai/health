# 06-refine — Clinical Calm

## Visual Thesis

A precise, calm, clinical website with structured evaluation, clarity, and measured hierarchy.

## Colour Thesis

Ivory, pale sage, and ink dominate. Bronze appears as measurement marks, process numerals, and thin boundaries. Blush is barely used, only for soft help notes.

## Systems

- Header: `clinical-grid-header`
- Mobile: `clinical-list`
- Footer: `clinical-ledger`
- Homepage anatomy: `article-row`
- Internal page anatomy: `contact-bridge`
- Component strategy: axis-grid with whatsapp-strip calls to action
- CTA strategy: soft-pill
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 7, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Ivory clinical hero
2. Pale sage trust grid
3. Ivory service evaluation matrix
4. White/cream process table
5. Pale sage safety statement
6. Ivory FAQ/education panel
7. Cream expectations ledger
8. White journal reading row
9. Sage consultation strip
10. Ink/sage clinical footer bridge

## All Other Pages

Use clear mastheads, grid-based sections, structured ledgers, pale sage panels, and minimal decoration.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No botanical decoration
- No blush warmth dominance
- No magazine layering.
