# 31-evolve — Education-Led

## Visual Thesis

A learning-first website where education leads conversion.

## Colour Thesis

Paper ivory base. Sage topic clusters. Gold reading markers. Blush callouts. Footer becomes reading index.

## Systems

- Header: `editorial-index-header`
- Mobile: `dark-sheet`
- Footer: `editorial-bridge`
- Homepage anatomy: `axis-grid`
- Internal page anatomy: `softline-sequence`
- Component strategy: editorial-spread with single-rule calls to action
- CTA strategy: split-quiet
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 5, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Paper ivory education hero
2. Sage learning values
3. Cream topic route
4. Ivory consultation explainer
5. Blush learning callout
6. Sage knowledge cluster
7. Cream results education
8. Ivory journal library
9. Gold reading CTA
10. Sage reading footer

## All Other Pages

Use reading paths, topic clusters, educational side notes.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No conversion-first aggressive CTAs
- No dark luxury
- No visual clutter.
