# 31-evolve — Mirror Of Confidence

## Visual Thesis

Mira uses reflective, elegant, confidence led pacing with mirror and reflection metaphor.

## Colour Thesis

ivory, mist, sage, deep green

## Systems

- Header: `editorial-index-header`
- Mobile: `dark-sheet`
- Footer: `editorial-bridge`
- Homepage anatomy: `axis-grid`
- Internal page anatomy: `softline-sequence`
- Component strategy: editorial-spread with single-rule calls to action
- CTA strategy: split-quiet
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 5, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Mira hero promise in sage
2. Mira trust position in ivory
3. Mira care route in cream
4. Mira consultation path in gold
5. Mira treatment navigation in bronze
6. Mira human reassurance in dark
7. Mira education preview in sage
8. Mira responsible expectations in ivory
9. Mira contact bridge in cream
10. Mira closing identity in gold

## All Other Pages

Inner pages keep the same care voice while changing the density, introduction, navigation rhythm, and visual emphasis by page type.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Do not flatten the site into repeated centered card stacks
- Do not use gray placeholders
- Do not invent testimonials, guarantees, prices, results, or credentials
- Do not let desktop collapse into a narrow mobile column
