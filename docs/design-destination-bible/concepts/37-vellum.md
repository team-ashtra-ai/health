# 37-vellum — Line-Art Clinical Beauty

## Visual Thesis

Linha uses precise, artistic, line based pacing with botanical and face line art composition.

## Colour Thesis

ivory, ink, sage, gold

## Systems

- Header: `warm-conversation-header`
- Mobile: `route-index`
- Footer: `minimal-rule`
- Homepage anatomy: `atelier-frame`
- Internal page anatomy: `process-rail`
- Component strategy: wide-horizon with soft-pill calls to action
- CTA strategy: minimal-arrow
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 5, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Linha hero promise in sage
2. Linha trust position in ivory
3. Linha care route in gold
4. Linha consultation path in cream
5. Linha treatment navigation in dark
6. Linha human reassurance in ink
7. Linha education preview in sage
8. Linha responsible expectations in ivory
9. Linha contact bridge in gold
10. Linha closing identity in cream

## All Other Pages

Inner pages keep the same care voice while changing the density, introduction, navigation rhythm, and visual emphasis by page type.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Do not flatten the site into repeated centered card stacks
- Do not use gray placeholders
- Do not invent testimonials, guarantees, prices, results, or credentials
- Do not let desktop collapse into a narrow mobile column
