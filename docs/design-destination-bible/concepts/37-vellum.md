# 37-vellum — Paper Editorial

## Visual Thesis

A paper/editorial site with layered documents, fine rules, and reading-led structure.

## Colour Thesis

Layered ivory/cream/paper tones. Bronze document lines. Sage annotations. Blush editorial notes.

## Systems

- Header: `warm-conversation-header`
- Mobile: `route-index`
- Footer: `minimal-rule`
- Homepage anatomy: `atelier-frame`
- Internal page anatomy: `process-rail`
- Component strategy: wide-horizon with soft-pill calls to action
- CTA strategy: minimal-arrow
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 5, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Paper editorial hero
2. Cream document trust
3. Ivory route document
4. Sage annotation process
5. Blush editorial note
6. Cream education folio
7. Ivory results ledger
8. Sage journal archive
9. Bronze document CTA
10. Paper/sage footer

## All Other Pages

Use document-like sections, side notes, and paper layering.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No glossy spa styling
- No dark luxury dominance
- No generic card grid.
