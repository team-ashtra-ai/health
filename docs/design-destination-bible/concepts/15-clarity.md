# 15-clarity — Readability First

## Visual Thesis

A high-legibility content-first site where every section is easy to read and scan.

## Colour Thesis

Ivory/cream base. Ink text. Sage section markers. Minimal bronze. Blush appears only in small help/care notes.

## Systems

- Header: `studio-ledger-header`
- Mobile: `rule-drawer`
- Footer: `route-directory`
- Homepage anatomy: `shape-field`
- Internal page anatomy: `softline-sequence`
- Component strategy: contact-bridge with gold-mark calls to action
- CTA strategy: ledger-button
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 7, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Ivory clarity hero
2. Cream plain-language values
3. White service index
4. Pale sage process explanation
5. Ivory safety statement
6. Cream FAQ education
7. White results explanation
8. Pale sage journal index
9. Ivory contact strip
10. Ink/sage readable footer

## All Other Pages

Prioritise readable line lengths, strong contrast, and clear headings.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No decorative gradients
- No low-contrast blush text
- No visual clutter.
