# 11-bloom — Botanical Narrative

## Visual Thesis

A narrative botanical website where the page feels like a calm care story unfolding.

## Colour Thesis

Sage 100/200/300 dominate. Cream is secondary. Blush appears like a flower accent. Bronze/gold is natural and subtle.

## Systems

- Header: `editorial-index-header`
- Mobile: `dark-sheet`
- Footer: `editorial-bridge`
- Homepage anatomy: `axis-grid`
- Internal page anatomy: `softline-sequence`
- Component strategy: editorial-spread with single-rule calls to action
- CTA strategy: split-quiet
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 1, 6, and 8; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Sage botanical story hero
2. Cream value roots
3. Sage route garden
4. Ivory consultation pathway
5. Blush bloom reassurance
6. Sage education leaves
7. Cream results growth ledger
8. Ivory journal seeds
9. Sage/blush contact bloom
10. Botanical footer field

## All Other Pages

Use organic transitions and botanical CSS separators between all 10 sections.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No stock botanical images
- No dark luxury
- No rigid clinical grid.
