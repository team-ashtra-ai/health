# 48-signature — Soft Private Escape

## Visual Thesis

Isla uses private, gentle, retreat like pacing with calm private escape.

## Colour Thesis

ivory, mist, sage, sand

## Systems

- Header: `layered-magazine-header`
- Mobile: `botanical-panel`
- Footer: `quiet-signature`
- Homepage anatomy: `reading-path`
- Internal page anatomy: `technical-table`
- Component strategy: shape-field with inline-route calls to action
- CTA strategy: text-link
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 7, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Isla hero promise in sage
2. Isla trust position in ivory
3. Isla care route in cream
4. Isla consultation path in gold
5. Isla treatment navigation in dark
6. Isla human reassurance in bronze
7. Isla education preview in sage
8. Isla responsible expectations in ivory
9. Isla contact bridge in cream
10. Isla closing identity in gold

## All Other Pages

Inner pages keep the same care voice while changing the density, introduction, navigation rhythm, and visual emphasis by page type.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Do not flatten the site into repeated centered card stacks
- Do not use gray placeholders
- Do not invent testimonials, guarantees, prices, results, or credentials
- Do not let desktop collapse into a narrow mobile column
