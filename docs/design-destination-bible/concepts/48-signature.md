# 48-signature — Polished Brand Signature

## Visual Thesis

A polished signature brand site with distinctive header, portrait identity, and refined conversion flow.

## Colour Thesis

Balanced ivory, sage deep, bronze/gold, slight blush. Strong signature footer.

## Systems

- Header: `layered-magazine-header`
- Mobile: `botanical-panel`
- Footer: `quiet-signature`
- Homepage anatomy: `reading-path`
- Internal page anatomy: `technical-table`
- Component strategy: shape-field with inline-route calls to action
- CTA strategy: text-link
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 7, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Signature ivory/sage hero
2. Cream brand trust
3. Sage route signature
4. Blush consultation warmth
5. Deep sage brand statement
6. Ivory education section
7. Cream results guidance
8. Sage journal path
9. Gold signature CTA
10. Deep signature footer

## All Other Pages

Use polished brand marks, signature linework, and consistent premium finish.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No generic wellness template
- No excessive dark
- No weak footer.
