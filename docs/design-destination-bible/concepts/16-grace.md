# 16-grace — Feminine Editorial

## Visual Thesis

A graceful feminine editorial site with soft curves, refined typography, and light movement.

## Colour Thesis

Ivory, blush 100/200, and soft sage dominate. Bronze is fine and elegant. Dark is rare.

## Systems

- Header: `clinical-grid-header`
- Mobile: `clinical-list`
- Footer: `clinical-ledger`
- Homepage anatomy: `evidence-ledger`
- Internal page anatomy: `technical-table`
- Component strategy: axis-grid with whatsapp-strip calls to action
- CTA strategy: soft-pill
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 5, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Ivory/blush editorial hero
2. Soft sage values
3. Cream route sweep
4. Blush consultation aside
5. Ivory graceful reassurance
6. Sage education section
7. Cream results note
8. Blush journal preview
9. Bronze-line feminine CTA
10. Soft sage footer bridge

## All Other Pages

Use gentle curves, refined typographic rhythm, and blush/sage alternation.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No dark heavy hero
- No clinical grids
- No generic rounded cards.
