# 16-grace — Feminine Botanical Bloom

## Visual Thesis

Bloom uses soft botanical growth pacing with blooming visual motif.

## Colour Thesis

blush, sage, ivory, rose beige

## Systems

- Header: `clinical-grid-header`
- Mobile: `clinical-list`
- Footer: `clinical-ledger`
- Homepage anatomy: `evidence-ledger`
- Internal page anatomy: `technical-table`
- Component strategy: axis-grid with whatsapp-strip calls to action
- CTA strategy: soft-pill
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 5, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Bloom hero promise in dark
2. Bloom trust position in bronze
3. Bloom care route in sage
4. Bloom consultation path in ivory
5. Bloom treatment navigation in cream
6. Bloom human reassurance in gold
7. Bloom education preview in dark
8. Bloom responsible expectations in bronze
9. Bloom contact bridge in sage
10. Bloom closing identity in ivory

## All Other Pages

Inner pages keep the same care voice while changing the density, introduction, navigation rhythm, and visual emphasis by page type.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Do not flatten the site into repeated centered card stacks
- Do not use gray placeholders
- Do not invent testimonials, guarantees, prices, results, or credentials
- Do not let desktop collapse into a narrow mobile column
