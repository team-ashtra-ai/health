# 32-serene — Spa Calm

## Visual Thesis

A refined spa-calm site with slow rhythm and understated visuals.

## Colour Thesis

Soft sage, cream, blush 50/100, gentle gradients. Minimal dark. CTAs soft sage or blush.

## Systems

- Header: `botanical-stem-header`
- Mobile: `salon-overlay`
- Footer: `magazine-index`
- Homepage anatomy: `folio-stack`
- Internal page anatomy: `technical-table`
- Component strategy: route-ledger with split-quiet calls to action
- CTA strategy: gold-mark
- Image strategy: Selective full transparent portraits in sections 2, 6, and 9; object-fit contain only.

## Homepage Colour Sequence

1. Soft sage spa hero
2. Cream calm trust
3. Blush gentle route
4. Ivory consultation calm
5. Sage reassurance band
6. Cream education flow
7. Blush results note
8. Ivory journal calm
9. Soft sage CTA
10. Sage footer

## All Other Pages

Use slow full-width bands and quiet imagery.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No clinical tables
- No high-contrast editorial
- No busy grids.
