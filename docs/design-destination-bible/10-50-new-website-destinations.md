# 50 New Website Destinations

## 01-inspire — Dark Premium Editorial

A confident dark editorial magazine site. It should feel like a luxury independent publication translated into a premium consultation-led care website.

Colour: Deep sage and ink dominate. Ivory appears as controlled reading relief. Bronze appears as vertical rules, underlines, section numbers, and quiet CTA detail. Blush is almost absent.
## 02-empower — Botanical Confidence

A confident botanical website with organic structure, not a soft spa cliché. The site should feel natural, clear, and quietly strong.

Colour: Sage 100/200/300 dominate. Ivory and cream provide calm negative space. Bronze/gold are tiny botanical line accents. Blush appears in one or two human warmth sections only.
## 03-enhance — Gold-Line Minimalist

A precise ivory minimalist website built around fine bronze/gold rules, quiet spacing, and sharp editorial restraint.

Colour: Ivory and cream dominate. Gold/bronze fine lines carry the identity. Sage appears only in text, hover, or small quiet sections. Blush appears as one pale separator only.
## 04-renew — Deep Sage Luxury

A dramatic deep sage luxury site with fewer, larger, stronger sections. It should feel immersive and high-end.

Colour: Deep sage and ink dominate. Gold and bronze are stronger than in Inspire. Ivory is used for contrast panels. Blush is almost absent.
## 05-elevate — Warm Consultation Studio

## 06-refine — Clinical Calm
