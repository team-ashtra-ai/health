02

What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on your device when you visit a website. They can help a page work, retain a choice, recognise a browser, or produce information about use of a service.

This site may also use technologies with a similar function, including localStorage (browser local storage), pixels, identifiers, and scripts. In this policy, “cookies” includes these technologies when they serve an equivalent purpose. “First-party” cookies are set by this website’s domain; “third-party” cookies are set or accessed by external providers.

03

Support security, navigation, technical operation, and storage of your privacy choices. They cannot be switched off in the panel because the site needs them to perform an action you requested.

Preferences

May remember language, display, or other options you select. They remain off until you allow them and can be withdrawn at any time.

Analytics

Measure visits, sessions, pages, interactions, and performance so we can understand and improve the site. Google Analytics should only be used after you allow this category.

Allow embedded resources or third-party features to load. If enabled, the provider may receive technical data such as your IP address, browser, visited page, and time.

Advertising: this website does not declare an active behavioural advertising category and keeps advertising storage, ad personalisation, and ad user-data signals denied. If this practice changes, this policy and the preference panel must be updated before use.

Data processed and purposes

Depending on the category you allow, data may include: IP address and approximate location derived from it; device type, operating system, browser, language, resolution, and time zone; visited URL, previous page, date, time, duration, click, scroll, and successful form-submission events; campaign parameters; cookie, session, or browser identifiers; and your consent choice.

06

Legal grounds and your consent

Strictly necessary features are used to deliver the page, maintain security, record your choice, and perform actions you request, in accordance with the principles of Brazil’s General Data Protection Law (LGPD). Preference, analytics, and external-media cookies rely on your freely given, informed, and unambiguous consent.

You may reject all optional categories without losing access to the main content. Refusal does not prevent you from requesting care, although a particular external feature may not work. Withdrawal applies to future use and does not make processing carried out while your earlier consent was valid unlawful.

Third parties and international transfers

If analytics is allowed, technical and usage data may be sent to Google Analytics, provided by Google LLC and its group companies. If external media is enabled, data may be transmitted to the provider identified on the embedded feature. Providers may act as processors or independent controllers depending on the service and their terms.

Because global providers maintain infrastructure in different countries, data may be processed outside Brazil. Where this occurs, applicable legal and contractual safeguards for international transfers and security measures consistent with the LGPD are used. External links, WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Reviews, and third-party pages have their own policies; opening an external link does not mean Franciele Sofiati controls that other site’s cookies.
