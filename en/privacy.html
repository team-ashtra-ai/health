This document brings together appointment conditions, consent, authorisations and privacy information related to Franciele Sofiati aesthetic care.

It should be read carefully. The registration form records acknowledgement and acceptance of the points that apply to the selected procedure.

1. Controller and scope

This Privacy, LGPD and Digital Communication Policy describes how Franciele Sofiati processes personal data and sensitive personal data connected with registration, booking, care, consent, communication, billing, photographic records and follow-up of aesthetic procedures.

This policy applies to data provided by the client in forms, messages, WhatsApp, email, in-person care, digital platforms and other means used to organise care.

2. Personal data processed

Data may include name, CPF, identification document, phone, WhatsApp, email, date of birth, address, city, desired procedure, treated area, agreed date and time, payment receipts, exchanged communications and administrative information necessary for care.

When necessary for safety, record keeping and follow-up, photographs, before-and-after images and voluntarily provided health-related information may also be processed.

3. Purposes of processing

Data is used for client identification, age and identity confirmation, booking, procedure communication, pre- and post-procedure guidance, receipts, billing, administrative organisation, consent records, legal and professional obligations and rights defence.

Image or testimonial data is used for marketing only when there is specific, free and highlighted authorisation.

4. Legal basis, sensitive data and consent

When sensitive data is involved, processing occurs for care safety, follow-up, professional records, legal obligations and regular exercise of rights, as applicable.

Promotional communication consent is separate from necessary care communication.

5. Photography and marketing

Technical records may be used for internal follow-up, progress comparison, care documentation, procedure safety and rights defence.

Use of images, videos, before-and-after material or testimonials on social media, websites, advertisements, promotional materials or portfolios is optional and requires specific authorisation.

Refusing marketing authorisation does not prevent assessment, booking or the procedure, provided the professional considers care technically safe.

6. Storage, providers and sharing

Data may be stored in digital systems, form platforms, cloud storage, WhatsApp, email, scheduling, financial systems, payment platforms, administrative files or other secure means adopted by the professional or clinic.

Access is restricted to the responsible professional and people or providers necessary to the operation. The professional or clinic does not sell personal data.

The professional or clinic does not sell personal data.

7. Retention and security

Data is kept for the time necessary for care, legal, regulatory and professional obligations, regular exercise of rights, client safety and care documentation.

Data used exclusively for marketing will cease to be used for new publications after consent withdrawal, subject to technical, legal and third-party limits.

8. Data subject rights

The data subject may request access, correction, clarification, deletion where applicable, consent withdrawal and information about sharing through the official channel: 43 99104-3536 and sofiatimendonca@gmail.com.

Requests are reviewed according to applicable law, professional obligations, recordkeeping needs and regular exercise of rights.

9. Complications and essential communication

Complications, unexpected reactions, intense discomfort, progressive worsening, signs of infection, burns, wounds, fever or allergic reaction must be reported immediately through the official channel.

The professional may request photos, reports, in-person return, reassessment or referral to medical or urgent care depending on severity.

10. Policy update

This policy was edited on July 21, 2026 and may be updated to reflect legal, technical, operational or professional changes.

The current version will be made available on the website and may be reviewed before form completion or care.
