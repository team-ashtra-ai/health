Section 3 of 21

Scheduling, booking fee, cancellation and no-show

To reserve the time, room, professional schedule and, where applicable, equipment, handpiece, machine or technology, a booking fee of 30% of the total procedure value may be charged. This fee is deducted on the appointment day when the client attends and the procedure is performed.

  • Rescheduling with 24 hours or more notice: the fee is kept as credit for a new date, subject to availability.
  • Cancellation with 24 hours or more notice: the fee may be converted into credit or refunded depending on the case and costs already assumed.
  • Less than 24 hours notice, delay that prevents safe care, or no-show: the fee may be retained.
  • Rented or reserved equipment may justify retention to cover committed costs.
  • Cancellation by the professional for technical, sanitary, ethical or safety reasons allows rescheduling, credit or refund of the amount corresponding to the procedure not performed.
