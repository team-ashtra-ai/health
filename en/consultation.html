A note from Franciele

Aesthetic care is also care for confidence.

The best answer is not always the best-known procedure or the newest treatment. Each person has different features, goals and expectations.

My commitment is to offer a careful assessment, explain the available options with transparency and recommend only what genuinely makes sense for you.

I want you to leave the consultation with clarity, safety and confidence to decide the next step.

— Franciele Sofiati

03

How the consultation works

What to expect from your appointment

  1. 01

    We talk

    You can calmly explain what motivated your enquiry and the results you hope to achieve.

  2. 02

    I carry out an assessment

    During the consultation, I carefully observe the area of interest and consider the factors that may influence treatment planning.

  3. 03

    I explain the possibilities

    We will discuss the most suitable options, expected benefits, limitations, recovery time when needed and any questions you have.

  4. 04

    You decide calmly

    After understanding the recommendations, you can decide when and how you would like to proceed, without any obligation to have a procedure.

04

What I consider when recommending treatment

Each plan is considered individually.

When preparing a recommendation, I consider several factors so the treatment is suited to your reality and your goals.

  • The characteristics of your skin, face, body or scalp
  • Your main concerns and expectations
  • Previous procedures
  • Your routine and recovery availability, when needed
  • Natural-looking results compatible with your profile
  • Safety at every stage of treatment

The goal is not to suggest more procedures, but to find the approach that offers the best balance between efficacy, naturalness and wellbeing.

05

Before the consultation

How you can prepare

No special preparation is required.

If you wish, you can share information that may help the assessment, such as previous treatments, products you currently use or photographs showing how your concern has changed.

If you have important commitments, travel or specific dates, it is also useful to mention them during the consultation so planning can be done appropriately.

The most important thing is to arrive calmly. We will take care of the rest together.

06

After the consultation

Clarity to decide the next step

At the end of the consultation, you will understand which options are most suitable for your case and can calmly decide how you would like to proceed.

When a treatment is recommended, I will explain each stage, including preparation, estimated number of sessions, necessary care and follow-up.

In some situations, the best path may be to wait, prepare the skin or simply monitor progress before beginning any procedure.

Each recommendation is made with your safety, wellbeing and goals in mind.

07

Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to know which treatment I want before the consultation?

No. Many people seek guidance precisely to understand which approach makes the most sense for their needs.

Does a consultation mean I will have a procedure the same day?

Not necessarily. Some treatments can be performed on the same day, while others require planning or preparation. Everything will be explained during the consultation.

Can I contact you on WhatsApp first?

Yes. If you have an initial question, our team can guide you on next steps and booking.

Is the appointment personalised?

Yes. Each consultation is individual and considers your features, goals and expectations.

How long does the consultation take?

The time can vary according to each case, allowing a careful assessment and space to answer all your questions.
