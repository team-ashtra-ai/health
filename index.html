Care philosophy

How the first step works

Three questions come before any procedure

The consultation filters interest into a responsible direction, especially when you are unsure whether the answer is skin care, laser, waiting, or a simpler first step.

What Franciele is trying to clarify

  • Suitability: whether your skin context, history, and comfort level support a route.
  • Timing: whether events, sun exposure, recovery windows, or routine consistency change the plan.
  • Expectation: what kind of result is reasonable, what may take staging, and what cannot be promised online.
