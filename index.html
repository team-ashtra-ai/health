08

Common questions

Questions before booking

Do I need to know which treatment to book?

No. Begin with the concern, change or question you would like to discuss. The consultation is used to assess suitability and explain the most appropriate options.

Can treatment happen during the first appointment?

Sometimes, but never automatically. It depends on the procedure, your health history, preparation requirements, available time and whether it is appropriate to proceed that day.

How many sessions will I need?

The number and spacing of sessions vary according to the concern, treatment response, chosen technology, home care and the result that can realistically be achieved.

What happens when a treatment is not suitable?

Franciele may recommend preparation, a different approach, more time, or evaluation by another qualified professional. A responsible plan may also mean deciding not to treat.
