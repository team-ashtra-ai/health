How the first step works

Three decisions happen before any procedure.

The consultation clarifies whether treatment is suitable, whether the timing is right, and whether the expected result is responsible to pursue.

  1. Your context is heard. Goals, history, current routine, sensitivity, and timing are part of the conversation.
  2. Options are filtered. Franciele explains what may fit, what may need preparation, and what does not make sense right now.
  3. Expectations are made practical. You understand possible limits, aftercare, and why individual response can vary.
