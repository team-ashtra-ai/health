06

RESPONSIBLE PAUSES

Sometimes the responsible answer is “not yet”

Recent sun exposure

Some laser, peel and pigmentation treatments need a different window.

Active irritation

Sensitive or inflamed skin may need support first.

Limited recovery time

Events, travel or work may make timing unsuitable.

Unclear cause

Some concerns need another professional evaluation.

Unrealistic expectation

Honesty matters when the expected change is not achievable.

Too much, too soon

A staged or simplified plan may be more appropriate.

Waiting is not the absence of care. Sometimes it is the most careful decision.
