What Franciele may assess

The first conversation turns interest into a clearer direction

Assessment is not only about choosing a procedure. It is about deciding what is appropriate for your real context.

Goals and timing

What you hope to improve, when you need to feel ready, and whether the schedule supports care.

Skin context

Routine, sensitivity, history, previous procedures, and current condition can change the plan.

Comfort and expectations

Questions about sensation, recovery, aftercare, and realistic outcomes are part of the conversation.

No rush

Requesting a consultation is not committing to a procedure

It is asking for professional clarity. If a route is appropriate, Franciele can explain why. If not, she can explain what should wait, change, or be prepared first.
