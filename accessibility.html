Moving through the site

The site includes skip navigation, semantic headings, visible focus states, responsive layouts, and a keyboard-accessible mobile menu.

Readable presentation

The design uses calm contrast, restrained motion, and responsive spacing so pages remain usable across mobile, tablet, and desktop.

  • Reduced-motion preferences are respected.
  • Buttons and links are designed for touch targets.
  • Content should remain readable without relying on decorative visuals.

Report an issue

If something is difficult to read, navigate, or use, contact the clinic with the page name and the issue you encountered.
