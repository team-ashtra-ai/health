Before booking

Start with the doubts most visitors have

These answers are direct on purpose. They should help you arrive calmer and more prepared.

Do I need to know what treatment I want?

No. It is enough to know what you would like to understand or improve. The consultation helps clarify whether skin care, laser, another route, or no immediate procedure makes sense.

Request consultation
How is suitability evaluated?

Franciele considers goals, skin context, relevant history, recent procedures, timing, comfort, and expectations before discussing options.

Read Care
Can results be promised in advance?

No. Individual response varies. A responsible conversation includes expected limits, aftercare, and factors that may influence progress.

How is laser suitability evaluated?

Laser guidance depends on skin context, timing, recent exposure, sensitivity, preparation, and aftercare needs.

What if my concern is texture, tone, or sensitivity?

Skin concerns are evaluated in context. Routine, consistency, previous treatments, and tolerance can all influence the plan.

Skin guidance
Can I ask about discomfort or downtime?

Yes. Comfort, downtime, and aftercare are appropriate consultation topics and should be understood before decisions.

Prepare

What should I bring to consultation?

Bring your main questions, current routine, relevant history, recent procedures, and any timing needs. You do not need to arrive with all answers.

  1. Your main concern. Describe what bothers you or what you want to understand.
  2. Your current routine. Products, consistency, sensitivity, and recent changes can matter.
  3. Your timing. Events, travel, sun exposure, and recovery windows can affect the plan.
