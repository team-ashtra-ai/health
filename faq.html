Starting point

Do I need to know what treatment I want?

No. It is enough to know what you would like to understand or improve. The consultation helps clarify whether skin care, laser, another route, or no immediate procedure makes sense.

  1. Share your concern.
  2. Ask your questions.
  3. Let evaluation guide the next step.

Suitability

How is suitability evaluated?

Franciele considers goals, skin context, health history, recent procedures, timing, comfort, and expectations before discussing options.

Results

How should results be understood?

No. Individual response varies. A responsible conversation includes expected limits, aftercare, and factors that may influence progress.

No. Laser guidance depends on skin context, timing, recent exposure, sensitivity, preparation, and aftercare needs.
