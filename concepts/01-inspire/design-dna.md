# 01 Inspire Design DNA

Concept name: Inspire

Visual idea: editorial diagnosis journey translated into a Sofiati visual world, using pressed botanical editorial as the governing asset language.

Asset idea: Local assets combine the approved logo, FS monogram, signature mark, a custom Franciele portrait treatment, botanical dividers, service visuals, form framing and an icon pack.

Colour usage idea: Sage and ivory hold the quiet clinical base, bronze and champagne gold draw attention to credentials, dividers and micro-details, and deep green-black gives professional contrast.

Typography idea: Elegant serif display rhythm for trust and editorial calm, paired with restrained sans-serif labels for clinical clarity.

Navigation idea: split masthead with clinical proof rail on desktop and chaptered fullscreen drawer on mobile, with botanical background assets and a small portrait cue in the menu.

Hero idea: The first viewport mixes the existing image-led Sofiati composition with the FS monogram, a custom botanical accent and a concept-specific portrait module for Franciele.

Doctor portrait idea: arched ivory portrait with bronze pressed-leaf edge, generated from `brand identity/Dr Fran.png` and saved as `assets/portrait/franciele-portrait-inspire.webp`.

Botanical/gold idea: gold leaf divider appears in the hero, section strip, mobile menu, form and footer.

Icon idea: A custom pressed botanical editorial SVG icon pack covers consultation, laser, skin, care, evaluation, safety, aftercare, results, journal, mission, values, contact, technology, credentials, WhatsApp and back to top.

Animation idea: line reveal controls strip dividers, icon hover behaviour, portrait movement and scroll CSS variables.

Form idea: minimal ivory form with a botanical frame, focus glow, privacy acknowledgement, honeypot, loading, success and error states.

Footer idea: business-card footer using the white logo, monogram wreath, public contact routes and Londrina, PR only.

How this concept differs from the other 49: It is identified by `pressed botanical editorial`, portrait treatment `arched ivory portrait with bronze pressed-leaf edge`, motion `line reveal`, form `minimal ivory form` and footer `business-card footer`. The HTML, CSS and JS include unique markers for this concept.
