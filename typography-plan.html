01 · The opportunity

The foundation is right.
The expression needs restraint.

The website already pairs an editorial serif with a practical sans serif. The next step is to make that idea consistent, intentional and unmistakably Franciele.

Today, overlapping scales, repeated uppercase labels, oversized headings, small utility text and frequent italics dilute hierarchy. The plan keeps the existing pages and content intact while giving every text element a clear role.

06 · Rules of restraint

Premium is a result of control

Headings

  • H1 normally fits within three mobile lines
  • Major H2 headings use two to four lines
  • No forced one-word line stacks
  • Sentence case for informational headings

Body copy

  • 58–68ch standard measure
  • 62–66ch for Journal articles
  • 1.6–1.75 line height
  • Short, left-aligned paragraphs

Labels

  • Uppercase only for genuine categories
  • 0.06–0.09em maximum tracking
  • Never above every heading
  • 14px minimum for practical text

Quotations

  • One principal quote per standard page
  • Italics only for short passages
  • No oversized decorative marks
  • Clear sans-serif attribution

Buttons

  • Sans serif, sentence case
  • Two to five words preferred
  • Same size across priorities
  • 44px minimum tap target

Forms

  • Visible labels above every field
  • 16px input text prevents mobile zoom
  • Readable privacy and helper copy
  • Respectful, direct error messages

08 · Implementation roadmap

Five deliberate phases

  1. 01

    Consolidate foundations

    Self-host two families, remove the cursive stack and replace overlapping variables with semantic roles.

  2. 02

    Repair shared components

    Unify navigation, buttons, forms, labels, cards, quotations, captions and utility interfaces.

  3. 03

    Apply by page family

    Start with Journal and homepage, then work through editorial, treatment and practical page groups.

  4. 04

    Complete the bilingual pass

    Review Portuguese and English together for natural casing, punctuation, line length and professional information.

  5. 05

    Verify every viewport

    Test 360 through 1440px, 200% zoom, contrast, image crops and every public route before release.
