Moving por meio de the site

The site includes skip navigation, semantic headings, visible focus states, responsive layouts, e a keyboard-accessible mobile menu.

Readable presentation

The design uses calmo contrast, restrained motion, e responsive spacing so pages remain usable across mobile, tablet, e desktop.

  • Reduced-motion preferences are respected.
  • Buttons e links are designed para touch targets.
  • Content should remain readable without relying on decorative visuals.
