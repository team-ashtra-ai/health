How the first step works

Three perguntas come antes de qualquer procedimento

The consulta filters interest into a responsável direction, especially when you are unsure whether the answer is pele cuidado, laser, waiting, ou a simpler first step.

What Franciele is trying para clarify

  • Adequação: whether your pele contexto, history, e conforto level support a route.
  • Tempo: whether events, sun exposure, recovery windows, ou routine consistency change the plan.
  • Expectation: what kind de result is reasonable, what may take staging, e what cannot be promised online.
