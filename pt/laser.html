  1. Check the contexto. Pele condition, recent sun exposure, sensitivity, e prior treatments matter.

Adequação checklist

Laser planejamento should name the variables

The goal is not para make laser sound dramatic. It is para understand whether the tempo, pele condition, e cuidados posteriores responsibilities are appropriate.

Exposure e tempo

Recent sun exposure, travel, outdoor plans, events, e recovery windows can change when laser makes sense.

Sensation e downtime

Perguntas about conforto, redness, e cuidados posteriores should be discussed antes de treatment.

Expected response

Resultados depend on indication, sessions, consistency, e individual pele response.

Preparo e cuidados posteriores

A laser plan includes what happens around the appointment

The responsável conversation includes what para avoid before, how para protect the pele after, e what signs ou sensations should be expected ou reported.

Perguntas worth asking

  • Before: what routine, exposure, ou pele condition could make waiting wiser?
  • During: what sensation is expected, e how will conforto be handled?
  • After: what protection, recovery cuidado, e follow-up tempo are needed?
