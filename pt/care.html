Cuidado filter

What is clarified antes de a route is suggested

  • What you want para improve and what would feel like a responsável result.
  • What your pele can support based on history, routine, sensitivity, e tempo.
  • What should wait because preparo, cuidados posteriores, ou expectativas need adjustment first.

Consulta sequence

A calmo sequence, not a sales script

The cuidado process is designed para make decisions safer, clearer, e more individual. It gives space para uncertainty, perguntas, e profissional restraint.

  1. Listen first. Your goals, concerns, conforto level, e prior experiences are part de the plan.
  2. Evaluate contexto. Pele condition, routine, tempo, exposure, e adequação shape what can be discussed.
  3. Explain the route. Preparo, cuidados posteriores, expected limits, e alternatives are clarified antes de a decision.

Responsável filtering

Not every option is right para every moment

Good cuidado can include saying wait, prepare first, choose a gentler route, ou avoid a treatment entirely. That restraint protects confiança e makes recommendations feel authored rather than automatic.

What gets filtered

The plan becomes useful when the perguntas are concrete

The goal is para turn estético interest into practical profissional orientação, not para make every concern sound like it needs a procedure.

Pele e health contexto

Sensitivity, recent procedures, current routine, medication-adjacent history shared em consulta, e visible pele condition can affect adequação.

Tempo e social calendar

Preparo, downtime, sun exposure, travel, important dates, e cuidados posteriores need para fit the moment you are actually in.

Conforto e consent

Perguntas about sensation, boundaries, e pace belong antes de the decision.
