No. It is enough para know what you would like para understand ou improve. The consulta helps clarify whether pele cuidado, laser, another route, ou no immediate procedure makes sense.

Can resultados be promised em advance?

No. Individual response varies. A responsável conversation includes expected limits, cuidados posteriores, baseline, consistency, e factors that may influence progress.

What should I bring para consulta?

Bring your main perguntas, current routine, relevant history, recent procedures, e any tempo needs. You do not need para arrive com all answers.

  1. Your main concern. Describe what bothers you ou what you want para understand.
  2. Your current routine. Products, consistency, sensitivity, e recent changes can matter.
  3. Your tempo. Events, travel, sun exposure, e recovery windows can affect the plan.
