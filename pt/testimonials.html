Responsável proof

What patient feedback should show

Useful depoimentos speak para feeling heard, informed, respected, e professionally guided. They should not promise identical outcomes para other people.

  1. Approved first. Only client-approved language should appear on this página.
  2. Specific para experience. Feedback can describe listening, explanation, conforto, e follow-up.
  3. No universal claims. Resultados depend on assessment, adequação, consistency, e cuidados posteriores.
