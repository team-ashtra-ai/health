Consultation sequence

A calm sequence, not a sales script.

The care process is designed to slow decisions down enough to make them safer, clearer, and more personal.

  1. Listen first. Your goals, concerns, comfort level, and prior experiences are part of the plan.
  2. Evaluate context. Skin condition, routine, timing, and suitability shape what can be discussed.
  3. Explain the route. Preparation, aftercare, expected limits, and alternatives are clarified before a decision.

Responsible filtering

Not every option is right for every moment.

Good care can include saying wait, prepare first, choose a gentler route, or avoid a treatment entirely. That restraint protects trust and makes recommendations feel authored rather than automatic.
