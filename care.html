Process

A calm sequence, not a sales script

The care process is designed to slow decisions down enough to make them safer, clearer, and more personal.

  1. Conversation about goals and comfort.
  2. Evaluation of skin context and suitability.
  3. A plan only when an option makes sense.

Suitability

Not every option is right for every moment

Timing, skin condition, recent treatments, routine, and expectations can all influence whether a path is appropriate now.
